Why Armor for Construction

Debris and Impact Protection

Construction sites generate flying debris, dust, and particles every shift. Lucyd Armor meets ANSI Z87.1-2020 impact standards with anti-scratch coatings, so your eye protection keeps performing through demanding conditions.

Hands-Free Communication on Site

Coordinating across a job site means constant communication. Bluetooth 5.3 and built-in walkie-talkie let you take calls, talk to your crew, and access AI assistants without removing gloves or reaching for your phone.

Indoor-to-Outdoor Transitions

Moving between framing, interiors, and outdoor work means constant lighting changes. Photochromic lenses adapt automatically, so you never need to swap between clear safety glasses and tinted sunglasses.

Built for Long Shifts

8-hour battery life matches your full work day. TR090 Onyx frames are lightweight and durable, with adjustable silicone nose pads and no-slip flexible temples designed for all-day comfort under a hard hat.
