### Article 1.1: First-Time Setup Guide

**Title:** First-Time Setup Guide
**Category:** Getting Started
**Tags:** setup, unboxing, start

---

**What's in the Box**

- Lucyd smart glasses
- Magnetic charging cable
- Carrying pouch
- Quick start guide

**Step 1: Charge Your Glasses**

Connect the magnetic charging cable to the temples. The LED will light up:
- Red = Charging
- Green = Fully charged

Charge for at least 30 minutes before first use.

**Step 2: Turn On**

Press and hold the button on either temple for 2 seconds.

**Step 3: Pair with Phone**

1. Make sure Bluetooth is ON on your phone
2. On your glasses, hold the button for 5 seconds until you hear "pairing mode"
3. Find "Lucyd" in your phone's Bluetooth settings
4. Tap to connect - you'll hear "connected"

**Step 4: Download the Lucyd App**

Get the Lucyd app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android) for:
- Firmware updates
- Touch control customization
- PD measurement tool

**Step 5: Enjoy!**

Play music, take calls, or activate your voice assistant. Tap the temples to control playback.

**Title:** Bluetooth Pairing Guide

---

**First-Time Pairing**

1. Turn on your glasses (hold button 2 seconds)
2. Enter pairing mode (hold button 5 seconds) - you'll hear "pairing mode"
3. On your phone:
   - iPhone: Settings ‚Üí Bluetooth ‚Üí Select "Lucyd"
   - Android: Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí Bluetooth ‚Üí "Lucyd"
4. You'll hear "connected" when successful

**Automatic Reconnection**

After first pairing, your glasses will automatically connect when:
- Turned on near your paired phone
- Your phone's Bluetooth is enabled

**Pairing to a New Device**

1. On your old device, go to Bluetooth settings and "Forget" Lucyd
2. Put your glasses in pairing mode (hold button 5 seconds)
3. Select "Lucyd" on your new device

**Troubleshooting**

If pairing fails:
- Make sure Bluetooth is ON
- Stay within 3 feet during initial pairing
- Restart your phone
- Factory reset: Hold BOTH buttons for 10 seconds

**Title:** Bluetooth Won't Connect
**Category:** Troubleshooting
**Tags:** bluetooth, connection, pairing, troubleshoot

---

**Quick Checks**

- [ ] Bluetooth enabled on phone
- [ ] Glasses charged (20%+ recommended)
- [ ] Within 10 feet of phone

**Step 1: Forget & Re-pair**

1. Go to phone Bluetooth settings
2. Find "Lucyd" and tap "Forget" or "Unpair"
3. Turn off glasses (hold button 2 sec)
4. Turn on and enter pairing mode (hold 5 sec)

**Step 3: Factory Reset Glasses**

Hold BOTH temple buttons for 10 seconds until you hear a tone. Re-pair from scratch after reset.

- Move away from WiFi routers
- Turn off other Bluetooth devices nearby
- Try in a different location

Contact support with:
- Phone model and OS version
- When the issue started

### Article 2.2: No Audio / Sound Issues

**Title:** No Audio / Sound Issues
**Category:** Troubleshooting
**Tags:** audio, sound, volume, troubleshoot

---

**Quick Checks**

- [ ] Phone media volume turned up (not just ringer)
- [ ] Glasses volume up (tap right temple)
- [ ] Audio output set to "Lucyd" on phone
- [ ] Glasses connected via Bluetooth

**No Sound At All**

1. Verify Bluetooth connection
2. Play audio from different app

5. Re-pair with phone

- Move closer to phone (within 30 feet)
- Remove obstructions between glasses and phone
- Check for WiFi/Bluetooth interference
- Update firmware via Lucyd app

**Sound Quality Issues**

- Crackling ‚Üí Check Bluetooth range
- Quiet ‚Üí Increase both phone AND glasses volume
- Distorted ‚Üí Update firmware, try reset

### Article 2.3: Charging Problems

**Title:** Charging Problems
**Category:** Troubleshooting
**Tags:** charging, battery, power, troubleshoot

---

**LED Indicator Guide**

- üî¥ Red = Charging
- üü¢ Green = Fully charged
- No light = Not connected properly

**No LED When Plugged In**

1. **Check magnetic connection** - Pins should snap firmly to contacts on temples
2. **Clean contacts** - Gently wipe temple contacts with dry cloth, clean cable pins too
3. **Try different power source** - Different USB port, wall adapter, or computer
4. **Inspect cable** - Look for damage, bent pins, fraying

**LED On But Won't Charge**

1. Leave plugged in for 30+ minutes
2. May be deeply discharged - needs time
3. Try different power adapter (5V/1A minimum)

**Won't Hold Charge**

- Full charge should last 6-8 hours of playback
- Battery degrades over time (normal after 1-2 years)
- If new and won't hold charge ‚Üí file warranty claim

**Charging Specs**

- Full charge: ~1.5 hours
- Quick charge: 15 min = 2 hours playback

### Article 4.2: PD Measurement Guide

**Title:** PD (Pupillary Distance) Measurement Guide
**Category:** Prescriptions
**Tags:** PD, pupillary distance, measure

---

**What is PD?**

PD (Pupillary Distance) is the distance between your pupils in millimeters. It's essential for positioning your lenses correctly.

**Average PD:** 57-72mm for adults

**Method 1: Lucyd App** ‚≠ê Easiest

1. Download Lucyd app
2. Open "Measure PD" feature
3. Hold credit card to forehead (for scale)
4. Look at camera
5. App calculates automatically

**Method 2: Mirror + Ruler**

1. Stand 8 inches from mirror
2. Hold mm ruler against brow
3. Close RIGHT eye
4. Align 0mm with center of LEFT pupil
5. Close LEFT eye, open RIGHT
6. Read number at center of RIGHT pupil
7. That's your PD!

**Method 3: Ask Your Doctor**

Call your eye doctor - they have it on file from your exam.

**Method 4: From Old Glasses**

A local optician can measure PD from your existing glasses.

**Single vs Dual PD**

- Single PD: One number (e.g., 63)
- Dual PD: Two numbers (e.g., 31/32)

Either works!

### Article 7.2: Frame Sizing Guide

**Title:** Frame Sizing Guide
**Category:** Product Info
**Tags:** sizing, fit, size

**Lucyd Sizes**

| Size | Face Width | Temple Length |
|------|------------|---------------|
| Small | 130-135mm | 140mm |
| Medium | 136-142mm | 145mm |
| Large | 143-150mm | 150mm |

**Medium fits most adults!**

**How to Measure**

**From Current Glasses:**

Look inside the temple for numbers like: 52-18-140
- 52 = Lens width
- 18 = Bridge width
- 140 = Temple length

Face width = Lens + Bridge + Lens
Example: 52 + 18 + 52 = 122mm

**No Glasses to Measure?**

- Measure face at widest point (cheekbones)
- Or use a credit card:
  - Card = 86mm wide
  - Face 1.5 cards wide = Small
  - Face 1.6 cards wide = Medium
  - Face 1.7+ cards wide = Large

**Title:** Battery & Charging Specs
**Category:** Product Info
**Tags:** battery, charging, specs

---

**Battery Life**

| Usage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Music/Podcasts | 6-8 hours |
| Phone Calls | 4-5 hours |
| Mixed Use | 5-6 hours |
| Standby | Up to 2 weeks |

**Charging**

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Full Charge | ~1.5 hours |
| Quick Charge | 15 min = 2 hrs playback |
| Connector | Magnetic pins |

**Charging Tips**

- Use any USB power source (5V/1A minimum)
- Magnetic cable snaps to temple contacts
- LED red = charging, green = full
- Don't charge while wet

**Maximize Battery**

- Lower volume when possible
- Stay close to phone (reduces power)
- Turn off when not in use
- Avoid extreme temperatures
